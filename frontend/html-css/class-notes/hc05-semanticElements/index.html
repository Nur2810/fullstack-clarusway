History of Music

Music has been a part of human culture for thousands of years. From ancient instruments to modern digital music, it has evolved tremendously over time.

The first musical instruments date back to prehistoric times, and various cultures have contributed to the diverse world of music we enjoy today.

  • Medieval (500-1400)
  • Renaissance (1400-1600)
  • Baroque (1600-1750)
  • Classical (1750-1820)
  • Romantic (1820-1900)
  • Modern (1900-present)

Music is a collection of coordinated sound or sounds. Making music is the process of putting sounds and tones in an order, often combining them to create a unified composition. People who make music creatively organize sounds for a desired result, like a Beethoven symphony or one of Duke Ellington's jazz songs.
